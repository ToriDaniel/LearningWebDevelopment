Links

Uses

Links are used to:

  • move from one webpage to another
  • switch between pages of the same website
  • jump to a different area of a webpage

Synthax

The address in the quotes after href= is the location of the image that you would like to use.

Tip: One of the common mistakes that my partner and I made when adding the image's address is just putting the name of the image without ensuring that the image is within the same folder as the .html file.
